The editor is actually fake, so you really can't do anything with it. It's basically just an interface with clickable buttons that do nothing. We threw it together to show how contextualized UI help looks. Just imagine how much cooler it would look in your application, though!

It was burgundy in the editor but now it's orange

So, to read your email in the editor:

  1. You can't.
  2. Go to another editor.
    Thunderbird isn't perfect, but it's free so maybe try that.
  3. Configure everything.
  4. Fail.
  5. Take a deep breath.
  6. Go get coffee.
  7. Try again.
  8. Succeed.

    You can now read your email. Click the message name to display it.
