How to read an email

There are three ways to read your email:

  • in the email client
  • in a browser
  • on paper.

For a guilt-free email experience, we recommend the first two methods.

Read an email with the editor

To read an email in the editor:

  1. Click the email to open the Message panel.
  2. Open eyes.
  3. Apply eyes to screen.
    Note: if eyes are missing or otherwise deficient, you can substitute screen reading software.
  4. Read the email.
  5. Go to print.
  6. Feel guilty about polar bears.
  7. Cancel printing.

Read an email in a browser

To read an email in a browser:

  1. Open a browser.
    We recommend Google Chrome.
  2. Go to https://mail.totallyrealdomain.com/.
  3. Click the email to open the Message panel.
  4. Open eyes.
  5. Apply eyes to screen.
    Note: if eyes are missing or otherwise deficient, you can substitute screen reading software.
  6. Read the email.
  7. Go to print.
  8. Feel guilty about polar bears.
  9. Cancel printing.
