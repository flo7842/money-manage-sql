Write an email using a keyboard

Using a keyboard is the simplest and most common way to type words in a computer. It is our recommended method.

To write an email using a keyboard, you need:

  • at least one hand
  • a keyboard
  • basic knowledge of grammar and punctuation.

Once you have all you need:

  1. Click in the edition field.
  2. Start typing.
  3. Stop at some point.
  4. Click Send.
